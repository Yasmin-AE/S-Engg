About Us

Established in 1987, S Engineering is a leading manufacturer of electrical motor shafts and pump shafts. Under the expert guidance of Mr. Abdul Rahiman Imam Shaikh, we’ve earned a reputation for delivering precision-engineered products with reliable performance. Our ISO 9001:2008 certification from MOODY INTERNATIONAL, U.K., underscores our commitment to quality. We also offer CNC machining with cylindrical grinding and keyway facilities, ensuring that we meet our clients’ needs with efficiency and excellence.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in precision-engineered motor and pump shafts, known for quality and innovation.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality products that meet our clients' needs.
To build lasting relationships through exceptional service and timely delivery.
To foster innovation and teamwork within our organization.

Operations

Turning

Precision machining of motor and pump shafts using CNC technology for accurate dimensions.

Key-Way

Specialized keyway cutting on shafts to ensure secure fitting and reliable performance.

Grinding

High-precision cylindrical grinding for smooth finishes and tight tolerances on shaft surfaces.

Impeller for Water Pump

Expertly manufactured and machined impellers for efficient water pump performance.
